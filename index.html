Products of violence

The state of drugs, rape
and murder in South Africa's
schools

There are over one million crimes reported every year to more than 1,000 police stations across the country. The numbers are colossal and with every crime there is a victim â€” most South Africans have been victims. The Mail & Guardian Data Desk analysed the past five years of SAPS crime statistics to find out where rape, murder, and drug-related crimes were reported the most. The data paints a very violent picture in the Western Cape and KwaZulu Natal. But the data also points to small, quiet areas in Limpopo with a serious problem in violating women and children.
